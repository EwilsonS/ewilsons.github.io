About me

So who am I? I would describe myself as a thinker. Learning new things is exciting to me, personal growth is essential. While my education and occupational history are diverse, the need to set and achieve goals to become better remain constant.

After about eight years in my previous career, I hit a plateau. I stopped growing and that’s a problem. Web development was always a thing I did in the background. Pursuing it professionally seemed an impossible feat. Little did I know, coding is just like learning a language, right? Slowly but surely I inched my way to web dev’s door and let myself in. Once I got my footing, I enrolled in the University of North Carolina’s Coding bootcamp to tie it all together for me. Here I am.

I specialize in working with the MERN (mongoDB, express, react and node) stack. Also, I’m a champion of modern methodologies/conventions such as Agile and MVC. Let's connect! I am confident that you will be pleased with my work ethic, knowledge and enthusiasm.
