Crystal Game by Evan Wilson

You will be give a random number when the game starts.

There are four crytals below. By clicking on a crystal,
you will be adding a certain amount of points to your
total score.

You win by matching your total score to the random number
displayed. You lose by exceeding that number.

The value of the crytals is hidden from you until they are
clicked and it changes value each new game.
