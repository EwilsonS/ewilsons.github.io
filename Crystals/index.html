Crystal Game by Evan Wilson

You will be given a random number when the game starts.

There are four crytals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will be adding a certain amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to the random number displayed. You lose by exceeding that number.

The value of the crytals is hidden from you, but they do not change umtil you've either won or lost.
